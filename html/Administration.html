Administration

Govt. College Chhachhrauli's Principal along with faculty members and other official staff members are solely responsible for overall supervision and maintenance of the college. Following are the activities which are being supervised by the college authorities:

  • Admission Process
  • Academic Affairs
  • Official Record Management
  • Maintenance and Audit of finance
  • Maintenance of Building
  • Safety and Security of girls Students
  • Public Affairs
  • Career Counselling
